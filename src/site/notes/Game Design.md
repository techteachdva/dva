Students will explore the art and science of creating board games. They will learn key design principles, delve into probability to shape gameplay, and develop skills in teamwork and collaboration. Throughout the course, students will prototype their own original board games and gain practical experience in preparing their designs for professional printing. By the end, each student will have created a fully realized, playable board game ready to share and showcase.
