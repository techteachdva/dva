URIEL

Turn prose into a play space: holon tokens, DM-ready notes (strong start → scenes → secrets), a player-facing rumor sheet, and an interactive graph—computed in your browser with Pyodide (no install, no upload).


    

    

    

  


  

    
URIEL is a hobby project: holonic tokenization, procedural names, and a kinds chart—not a commercial RPG product. Technical tab holds encodings; DM and Player tabs are table aids.
