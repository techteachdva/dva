v0.9.0 - Teeth & Bloodier Teeth (Difficulty + Combat Depth)
---------------------------------------------------------------------
DAMAGE NUMBERS SCALED UP ACROSS THE BOARD:
- New CHAMBER_DMG_SCALE table: [1.0, 1.18, 1.32, 1.45]. Every source of
  damage the player takes is multiplied by this based on which

- Climb debris raw damage numbers +~40%:
    rock   25 -> 34    bone  20 -> 28    tooth 18 -> 25
    goblin 22 -> 30    dagger 14 -> 20   sword 22 -> 30
    mace   16 -> 24    meat 10 -> 16
- Mace stun extended 1.3s -> 1.4s (it already hurts, leaning in).
- Bile-submersion HP drain 18 -> 24 /sec.
- Acid-gout base damage 16 -> 20 (braced 8 -> 11).
- Enemy melee attackDmg ranges bumped 25-40%, and each enemy gained a
  `heavyDmg` range for their new HEAVY SLAM move.
- Acid-timer corrosion in combat also scales with chamber.

ENEMIES NOW HAVE THREE ATTACK TYPES:
- JAB (default): the old wind-up / strike. Brace halves it.
- HEAVY SLAM: longer wind-up (1.6s + dodge window * 0.6), ~2x damage,
  and CRITICALLY: lane-dodging does NOT save you - brace is the only
  counter. Telegraphed with an ORANGE banner: "LANE DODGE WON'T SAVE
  YOU - BRACE (4)". Impact comes with a thicker flash, stronger
  particles, and heavier screen shake.
- TRIPLE STRIKE COMBO: short tell, then 3 hits in 0.84s. Each hit
  does 55% base damage. Brace covers each hit individually, so you
  need to TIME your brace to catch the last couple. Telegraphed with
  a YELLOW banner: "TRIPLE STRIKE x/3 - BRACE NOW (4)".
- Move selection is weighted: 62% jab / 20% combo / 18% heavy
  normally, shifting to 35% heavy / 30% combo / 35% jab in enrage.

ENRAGE PHASE:
- Every enemy has `enrageAt: 0.5` (HP threshold). When crossed, a
  one-shot "ENRAGED!" banner fires, screenShake(14), red particle
  burst, and a permanent [ENRAGED] tag appears by the enemy name.
- Enraged enemies get:
    + 20% damage on every attack
    + 30-35% faster turn cadence
    + Double acid gouts (2 different lanes simultaneously, 45% of
      the time)
    + Shifted move pool favoring heavy / combo

PERFECT BRACE COUNTER-ATTACK:
- If you press BRACE (4) within the last 0.5s of a tell, OR at any
  point during a combo sequence, you trigger a PERFECT GUARD.
- "PERFECT GUARD! Counter-attack is ready (+50% next hit)!" log.
- Next attack you land does x1.5 damage, logged as "COUNTER-STRIKE!"
  with a brighter enemyFlash + bigger shake.
- A glowing "COUNTER READY" indicator sits between enemy and player
  until you spend the counter.
- This gives the fight a read-and-punish rhythm: Spam brace too
  early and you waste it. Wait and you get rewarded.

UI CHANGES:
- Attack tell banner recolors based on move type (red jab / orange
  heavy / yellow combo) so move type is readable at a glance.
- Brace menu description now says "Reduce next hit. Time it late
  for +50% counter!".
- Combo state keeps the banner up for its entire duration, with a
  live "TRIPLE STRIKE n/3" counter so you can time the last brace.

v0.12.0 - The Big Replayability Update
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Five new pillars turn the fixed 10-minute run into a randomized,
build-shaping roguelite with meta-progression.

--- PILLAR 1: PACTS (between chambers) ---
- After every boss kill, before the next climb, a new PactScene
  offers 3 random "Pacts" to choose from. Elite kills buff this to 4.
- Every pact is a TRADEOFF (one pro + one con) so there are no
  dominant picks; choices shape the rest of the run.
- New file content/pacts.js with ~18 pacts across families. Examples:
      PACT_OF_VIPERS   +30% attack/special damage; HP max -15%
      FEATHER_CLOAK    +20% climb speed, faster hops; armor soak -15%
      IRON_GIZZARD     +40 HP max + full heal; climb speed -10%
      BILE_TONGUE      +50% acid-timer duration; -15% attack damage
      PERFECTIONIST    brace counter 1.5x -> 2.0x; base dmg -10%
      RING_FORGER      free Ring of Armor next climb; bile +15% faster
      PATIENT_BLADE    +25% crit chance; attacks cool 15% slower
      MARATHON_LUNGS   +12s acid timers; no mana regen between rooms
- Pact application mutates the player via applyPact(p, id) and
  tracks active pacts on p.pacts for UI + scoring. Pact modifiers
  live in p.pactMods and are consumed by both climb.js (bile rise,
  debris rate, climb speed, acid resist) and combat.js (damage,
  cooldowns, crit chance, counter multiplier).
- Flow: Climb -> Combat -> PactScene -> Transition -> Climb.

--- PILLAR 2: ELITE BOSS VARIANTS ---
- Each chamber rolls for an Elite variant (30/40/45/50/55/60% by
  chamber). Elites get +30% HP, +15% damage, and enrage at 60% HP.

=====================================================================
v0.13.0 - Into the Maw
=====================================================================
A brand-new FINAL CHAMBER, a brand-new FINAL BOSS, and a brand-new
way to play combat. Previously the Gullet was the top of the worm;
now you have to climb one level further - into THE MAW itself - and
face the worm's own TONGUE in a puzzle-combat showdown that trades
lane-hopping for mouse-aim precision.

--- THE MAW (new chamber, index 4) ---
- New chamber definition in content/chambers.js with a bright pink
  palette, shorter climb (1500 units - the final sprint), tight
  debris cadence, and a guardian flagged `wormTongue`.
- ClimbScene auto-decorates THE MAW with huge ivory FANGS along the
  left and right edges of the screen, drooling tinted saliva. The
  fangs are strictly cosmetic; the 5 climb columns are untouched.
- Chamber pacing is tuned so even Ironhide can reach the top if
  they keep moving - the bile bites harder but the peak is close.

--- THE WORM'S TONGUE (new final boss) ---
- New enemy definition `wormTongue` in content/enemies.js with 420
  HP, bite-hard attack/heavy damage ranges, custom flavor lines,
  and `art: "tongue"`. Matchup multipliers still apply (your
  weapon can be STRONG or WEAK against the tongue like any boss).
- New scene `scenes/tongueBoss.js` replaces the usual CombatScene
  exclusively for the Maw:
    * Dark maw interior with a full TEETH RING around the canvas
      (upper fangs down, lower fangs up, side fangs inward) and a
      central pulsing throat gradient.
    * The tongue is rendered as a pink serpentine body with 18
      shaded segments, a centerline groove, a wet highlight, and a
      bulbous forked tip that springs toward its strike target.
    * Progressive blood decals cluster around the tip as you hit
      it, and a rhythmic drip animation kicks in at low HP.
- Boss state machine: intro -> fight -> win. Victory skips the
  pact picker and goes straight to VictoryScene (this IS the win).

--- AIM-RETICLE PUZZLE COMBAT ---
- Mouse now controls a reticle drawn as a thin crosshair with a
  soft particle trail. Reticle color changes to signal state:
      GREEN  - inside the green AIM LOCK zone
      RED    - inside a red LASH DANGER zone
      IVORY  - neutral
- Green AIM LOCK ZONE: a drifting circle (~110px radius) that
  wanders around the tongue tip. Attacks only register when your
  reticle is inside it; attacking while unlocked produces a red
  MISS ring and a "MISS!" log line.
- Red LASH ZONES appear every ~3s (1.6s at Enrage). Each zone
  warns for ~1.25s, biased 60% toward your current reticle
  position so you can't just camp the center. If your reticle is
  still inside when the timer expires, the tongue strikes and you
  take damage; brace halves it, a Perfect Brace timed in the last
  0.35s readies a +50% counter on your next locked hit.
- Heavy CURL attack: an aim-ignoring full-screen tongue slam on a
  ~10s cadence. BRACE is mandatory - no amount of mouse skill
  saves you. Perfect-brace window applies the same way.
- Bullseye bonus: reticle within 30% of the zone center on a hit
  grants +25% damage. The floater turns green to confirm.
- Zone shrinks and speeds up when the tongue Enrages below 40%.
- A constant LOCKED/UNLOCKED banner sits above the reticle and a
  flashing context-hint strip advertises the next correct action
  (move mouse / BRACE / counter ready / etc.).

--- NEW KEYBIND LAYOUT ---
- Q / E / R / F are now full-fledged aliases for actions 1-4 in
  BOTH CombatScene and TongueBossScene:
      [Q] / [1] Attack
      [E] / [2] Special
      [R] / [3] Dodge Roll (+MP)
      [F] / [4] Brace
- The old 1-4 keys continue to work; you can mix them freely in
  the same fight. Combat log lines, context hints, the action
  panel, the in-combat help bar, and the telegraph banners all
  now show both aliases ("BRACE (F/4)", "Press [Q/1]", etc.).
- Left-click in the Tongue fight is also bound to Attack, so you
  can play the Maw entirely mouse-driven if you want.

--- INPUT ENGINE ---
- engine/input.js now accepts an optional canvas element. When
  provided:
      * mousemove is tracked at the WINDOW level and mapped into
        CANVAS-SPACE (1280x800) using the current bounding rect,
        so CSS-resized canvases still produce correct coordinates.
      * mousedown/mouseup are tracked as synthetic keys "Mouse0"
        (left), "Mouse1" (middle), "Mouse2" (right) so existing
        wasPressed() call sites can consume clicks with no new API.
      * The canvas's native right-click menu is suppressed.
- main.js wires the game canvas into Input at boot; game.canvas
  is also exposed on the game object for future scenes.
