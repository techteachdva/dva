v0.7.0 - The "Iron Finally Wins + Combat Has Teeth" Patch
---------------------------------------------------------------------
BILE / IRONHIDE FIX (the critical bug):
- Being submerged in the rising bile no longer kills instantly.
- Armor now acts as a bile-survival buffer: while submerged, armor
  corrodes at 1 point per second. Ironhide (60 armor) gets a full ~60
  seconds of grace to claw back above the surface, exactly matching
  their armor rating. Swiftfoot (0 armor) still drowns fast.
- Once armor is fully eaten by the bile, HP drains at 18/sec for a
  panicked "GET OUT NOW" moment with screen flash + blood particles.
- New on-submerge toast: "SUBMERGED! Armor buying you ~60s of grace."
  so the player actually SEES the mechanic working.
- Result: the Armored build can now reach the Gullet and win the
  game, not just dunk and die three pixels short of the finish line.

CLIMBING - WIDER, LONGER, MEANER:
- Climbing columns: 3 -> 5. Spread across 60% of screen width so
  the outer columns are a real hop away, not a flick.
- Chamber climb heights bumped 30-45% to draw out the tension:
    Stomach     1100 -> 1600 px
    Lower Gut   1300 -> 1900 px
    Heart-Hollow 1500 -> 2200 px
    Gullet      1700 -> 2500 px
- Bile rise rates dropped 30-35% so Ironhide can keep pace:
    Stomach     10 -> 6.5 px/sec
    Lower Gut   13 -> 8.5 px/sec
    Heart-Hollow 16 -> 11 px/sec
    Gullet      19 -> 13.5 px/sec
- Multi-column debris: each spawn has a chance to immediately
  trigger a second telegraph in a different column. Chance ramps
  per chamber (18% / 28% / 38% / 50%) for escalating panic.
- Debris interval windows tightened to match the bigger column grid.
- Column indicator at bottom now shows all 5 columns.

COMBAT - WEAPONS WITH PERSONALITY:
- NEW: weapon strength / weakness matchups against enemy art types.
  Devastating hits deal x1.6 damage (gold particles + "DEVASTATING!"),
  glancing blows deal x0.6 damage (blue particles + "GLANCING BLOW"):
    KEEN LONGSWORD : STRONG vs rotted husks, WEAK vs tentacles
    WARHAMMER      : STRONG vs toothed beasts, WEAK vs flesh horrors
    STAFF OF EMBERS: STRONG vs flesh horrors, WEAK vs bile oozes
    FROSTBITE WAND : STRONG vs bile oozes, WEAK vs toothed beasts
- The loadout-select screen now labels each weapon's matchups so
  you pick knowing what you're up against.
- First combat log line announces the matchup against the guardian.
- Enemy portrait now shows the matchup tag under its name.

COMBAT - LONGER, CHEWIER BATTLES:
- Enemy HP bumped 60-80% across the board so fights last 3-5
  meaningful exchanges instead of ending in 2 button mashes:
    Gullet Tentacle  65 -> 115
    Tooth Beast      80 -> 135
    Digested Goblin  60 -> 105
    Flesh Horror    110 -> 180
    Bile Elemental  130 -> 210
- Per-chamber scaling raised 0.12x -> 0.15x.
- Enemy melee wind-up cadence tightened from ~4-6s to ~2.8-3.8s
  so you can't just Brace every wind-up without giving up attacks.
- Acid-gout intervals tightened 20-30% so the dodge-lane game
  keeps pressure on between menu decisions.
- Weapon attack cooldowns nudged up 10-15% to defeat button mashing:
    Slash     0.55 -> 0.62s
    Smash     0.85 -> 0.95s
    Ember Bolt 0.48 -> 0.55s
    Ice Shard 0.40 -> 0.45s

COMBAT - JUICY VISUAL FEEDBACK:
- Enemy HP bar now animates smoothly: a dark-red underlayer snaps
  to the true HP for instant feedback, while the colored overlay
  lerps down for a satisfying tick-down.
- NEW: floating damage numbers pop off the enemy on every hit
  (gold for devastating, blue for glancing, white for neutral).
- NEW: persistent blood decals splatter onto the enemy model
  at random local positions with each hit. They stack, so by
  late-fight the guardian is visibly gore-soaked.
- NEW: global bloody sheen overlay on the enemy that intensifies
  as HP drops below full. Bile Elemental gets a sickly darkening
  instead of red so it reads correctly.
- NEW: at below ~45% HP, non-bile enemies drip visible blood
  drops off their body in a rhythmic animation.
- Stronger particle burst + screen shake on devastating hits.

v0.8.0 - Chamber Atmosphere + Falling Item Menagerie
---------------------------------------------------------------------
BACKGROUND NOW REFLECTS WHERE YOU ARE IN THE WORM:
- drawFleshBackground() accepts a per-chamber palette (deep / mid /
  bruise / bump). Each chamber ships its own, so the further you
  climb the worm the lighter and redder the chamber gets:
    Stomach      : blackish purple, barely any light (#08020a / #22072a)
    Lower Gut    : purplish maroon, a bit lighter (#1a0416 / #4a1040)
    Heart-Hollow : deep ventricle red (#2a0812 / #6a1024)
    Gullet       : reddish pink, daylight bleeding in (#3a0a1a / #9a2a3e)
- Combat scenes also pull their chamber palette now, so the
  sphincter gate matches the color of the chamber you fought through.

CHAMBER DIFFICULTY ACTUALLY RAMPS:
- New per-chamber `spawnCount`: 1 / 2 / 2 / 3. Each cycle fires that
  many telegraphs simultaneously across different columns.
- `multiDebrisChance` ramped to 18% / 30% / 45% / 55% for bonus
  spawns on top of the base count.
- Debris intervals tightened again in the back half of the game
  (Heart-Hollow 0.85-1.5s, Gullet 0.65-1.15s) to keep constant
  pressure even as the player learns the patterns.
- Net effect: Chamber 4 now feels like a falling-object hailstorm
  - which is exactly what a worm's gullet near daylight should.

NEW FALLING ITEMS - HAZARDS:
- ROCK (armor-absorb): classic crushing hit. Armor soak applies, so
  Swiftfoot (0 soak) eats the full 25 damage and Iron shrugs most
  of it off. Tank pips still eat whole hits.
- DAGGER (pierce, 14 dmg): sharp and nimble. Bypasses armor entirely
  and plants itself in HP. Telegraphs don't warn about type, so
  Iron's false sense of security can be deadly.
- SWORD (pierce, 22 dmg): same pierce rule, bigger hurt.
- MACE (armor-direct, 16 dmg): slams armor DIRECTLY instead of HP.
  If you have no armor it STUNS you for 1.3s (no climb, no hop,
  gentle slip) and taxes HP for 40% of its damage. Swift's recurring
  nightmare.
- MEAT CHUNK (flesh-only, 10 dmg): wet, gory splat. Ignored entirely
  if you have any armor (log says "SPLAT! Slides off your armor").
  Hurts a little if you're bare-skinned.
- Legacy bone / tooth / zombie-goblin entries kept and retyped as
  armor-absorb for consistency.

NEW FALLING ITEMS - POWER-UPS (rare, telegraphed in GOLD):
- FEATHER OF FLYING: instant +220px climb boost, trail of feathers,
  whoosh banner. Effectively saves ~1 second of climbing.
- CHEESEBURGER: heals 30 HP on catch. Does NOT touch armor. Drawn
  as a full stack (bun / lettuce / cheese drip / patty / seeded bun).
- RING OF ARMOR: VERY rare, glows on approach. Grants +50 armor
  (raising armorMax if needed) and a 50% armor-soak rating. For
  Swiftfoot it's game-changing - suddenly you have bile grace AND
  damage mitigation for the rest of the climb. Drawn as a haloed
  golden ring with a crimson jewel.

HOW THE DROPS WORK:
- Telegraphs are color-coded now: RED warning for hazards, GOLD
  glow + "+" icon for power-ups. You can instantly tell whether to
  dodge out of the lane or hop into it.
- Per-chamber powerUpRarity: 12% / 10% / 8% / 7% of spawns roll into
  the power-up pool. Inside that pool weights are 15 feather /
  12 burger / 2 ring - so rings appear maybe once or twice per run.

NEW VISUAL FEEDBACK:
- Each new item has a unique cel-shaded render: metallic daggers,
  full-size swords w/ pommel jewel, spiked mace with radial spikes,
  wobbling lobed meat chunk with bone nub, feather with curved
  quill + individual barbs, stacked burger, haloed armor ring.
- Power-ups have aurora halos in their respective colors so the
  eye is drawn to them even under cover.
- Ring-of-armor pickup triggers a golden shockwave ring expanding
  outward from the hero for ~1.2s.
- STUN state: golden pentagram stars orbit the hero's head, climb
  and hop inputs are locked, and climb progress slips gently until
  the stun wears off. Very readable "you got bonked" moment.
